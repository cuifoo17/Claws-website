About Usage

Usage is a measure of how much you interact with Claws. Think of it as an allowance for the period — it sets how much you can do with Claws before your limit resets at the start of the next one.

Only two things draw from your allowance: the messages you send Claws, and the voice readouts you use to hear his replies out loud. Longer messages tend to use a little more than shorter ones, but everyday chatting is light — I've optimized Claws as much as I can so you can talk to him as much as possible.

Every tier gives you the exact same Claws — the same companion, the same features. The only thing that changes as you move up is how much usage you get: higher tiers simply give you more room to chat.

Claws's memory also runs quietly in the background every so often, helping him remember what matters to you between conversations. This uses a little usage too, but far less than your actual messages — so it's not something you'll need to think about.
